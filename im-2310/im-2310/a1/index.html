Liquid Entropy

Liquid Entropy by Mika Chernov & Sinjin Hawke

is a visual synthesizer project by Fractal Fantasy that is described as an ‘exploration of hydrodynamics, fluid simulation, and psychedelic abstraction’. It features liquid-like shapes and colours that move and interact with each other.

I was drawn to the dynamic and fluid nature of the motion coming from the movement of my control. I enjoyed seeing the simulation of the malleable and constantly changing form of the metallic liquid. As well as the syncing with the audio effects.
